I am a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Division of Cardiovascular Medicine, Department of Internal Medicine, at [UC Davis Health](https://health.ucdavis.edu/), where I work with Dr. Martin Cadeiras and Dr. Vladimir Filkov (AI for Health Center). My research bridges computer science and cardiovascular medicine, with a focus on harmonizing, integrating, and analyzing large-scale clinical and research datasets.

My current work applies machine learning, geospatial analysis, and survival modeling to heart failure with preserved ejection fraction (HFpEF) — a heterogeneous syndrome that disproportionately affects women and has limited effective therapies. As a Fellow of the NIH-funded HeartShare consortium, I develop methods to phenotype and analyze HFpEF cohorts across multiple clinical trials and observational studies. As a Fellow of the AHA-funded SFRN UC Davis PRECISE Center, I integrate wearable biosignal, psychosocial survey data, and place-based environmental data into unified analytic frameworks.

Given the disproportionate burden of HFpEF and other chronic conditions on women's health, I am interested in applying computational modeling and new approach methodologies to study hormonal regulation and sex differences in cardiovascular disease.

I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Exeter (UK), where my doctoral work applied network science to understand coordination in population-based optimization algorithms. Before joining UC Davis, I was a postdoctoral researcher in digital humanities at the Institute of History of the Polish Academy of Sciences, and an AI Scientist at City Science, University of Exeter, working on human mobility and urban systems.

Research interests
======

- **Clinical data harmonization** — multi-cohort and multi-trial harmonization, common data elements, clinical-trial and EHR data
- **Machine learning & statistics** — clustering and phenotyping, survival analysis, individualized treatment-effect and causal-inference methods
- **Geospatial & exposomic analysis** — built-environment feature engineering, spatial clustering, neighborhood-level exposures and health
- **Clinical & biomedical NLP** — scientific text mining, named-entity resolution, large language models
- **Network science & optimization** — complex networks, swarm intelligence, bio-inspired metaheuristics
