Profile
======
Postdoctoral scientist bridging computer science and cardiovascular medicine, with a focus on harmonizing, integrating, and analyzing large-scale clinical and research datasets. Current work applies machine learning, geospatial analysis, and survival modeling to heart failure with preserved ejection fraction (HFpEF), integrating clinical-trial cohorts, wearable biosignals, psychosocial survey data, and place-based environmental data into unified analytic frameworks.

Research experience
======
* **Postdoctoral Scholar** — University of California, Davis (UC Davis Health), 2024–Present
  * Division of Cardiovascular Medicine (Dr. Martin Cadeiras) & AI for Health Center (Dr. Vladimir Filkov)
  * Cross-trial data harmonization (HeartShare consortium): harmonize and phenotype HFpEF patients across independent clinical-trial cohorts (TOPCAT, RELAX, NEAT-HFpEF, INDIE-HFpEF); develop individualized treatment-effect models
  * Geospatial and exposomic data integration: designed and led a pipeline integrating built-environment and neighborhood-level exposures at high spatial resolution across California; identified geographic HFpEF clusters (N = 6,702) with marked survival differences (adjusted HR ≈ 1.25)
  * Multimodal clinical data integration (PRECISE / SFRN center): integrate wearable-derived physiological signals, psychosocial survey instruments, and clinical cohort data, including circadian-rhythm analysis and standardized cardiovascular-health scoring
  * Clinical and biomedical NLP: apply natural language processing to biomedical literature to map and prioritize protein–disease associations in HFpEF
  * Collaborate with clinical cardiologists to align computational methods with clinical research questions; mentor data-science trainees

* **Postdoctoral Researcher, Digital Humanities** — Institute of History, Polish Academy of Sciences, Warsaw, Poland, 2023–2024
  * Applied NLP, large language models, and data-mining methods to large historical corpora; built structured, standardized datasets and authority dictionaries of people, places, and institutions, resolving duplicate records, name variants, and entity changes over time
  * Examined the role of rigorous data preprocessing and transparent documentation in ensuring result quality and reproducibility across data sources

* **AI Scientist (KTP Associate)** — City Science, University of Exeter, Exeter, UK, 2022–2023
  * Developed AI and network-science models for urban systems, including human-mobility analysis during the COVID-19 pandemic (partnership with Cuebiq Inc.) and analysis of targeted policing interventions (Devon & Cornwall Police)

* **Artificial Intelligence Engineer / R&D** — PickCells, Recife, Brazil, 2019
  * Built deep-learning methods for automated parasitological diagnostics and disease-vector image analysis

* **Software Engineer** — Motorola Mobility, Recife, Brazil, 2015–2017
  * Software engineering across analyst and engineering roles (internship to full engineer)
